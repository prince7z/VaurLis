Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to take out finance on my chosen car?

To apply for finance, you will typically need your driver's license, proof of income (like recent pay stubs or bank statements), and proof of residence (such as a utility bill). Having these documents ready will speed up the application process.

Can I get pre-approved for finance?

Yes, absolutely! We encourage customers to get pre-approved. You can fill out our secure online finance application to see what you qualify for before you even visit. This gives you a clear budget to work with.

Does Carbase negotiate on price?

We operate on a fixed-pricing model. This means the price you see is the price you pay, with no hidden fees. We believe this provides a transparent and haggle-free experience for all our customers.

What are our payment methods?

We accept several payment methods, including debit cards, bank transfers, and financing arranged through our approved lenders. Please note that we do not accept personal checks or cash for vehicle purchases.

Do we accept 3rd party finance lenders?

Yes, we are happy to work with your chosen bank or credit union. If you have pre-arranged financing from a third-party lender, please bring the necessary documentation, and our team will facilitate the process.
